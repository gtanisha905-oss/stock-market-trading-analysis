# Stock Market Trading & Analysis - Week 1

## DecodeLabs Internship

### Project Title
Technical Analysis of Reliance Industries Ltd.

### Stock
Reliance Industries Ltd. (NSE)

### Timeframe
Daily (6 Months)

### Indicators Used
- Exponential Moving Average (EMA 50)
- Exponential Moving Average (EMA 200)
- Relative Strength Index (RSI)

### Support Level
₹1280

### Resistance Level
₹1400

### Observations
- The stock is trading below the 50 EMA and 200 EMA.
- RSI is around 44, indicating neutral to slightly weak momentum.
- The overall trend is weak to bearish.
- ₹1280 is acting as a support level.
- ₹1400 is acting as a resistance level.

## How to Run

1. Open any charting platform (e.g., Chartink).
2. Search for **Reliance Industries Ltd. (NSE)**.
3. Select the **Daily (1D)** timeframe.
4. Apply the following indicators:
   - EMA 50
   - EMA 200
   - RSI (14)
5. Mark the Support and Resistance levels.
6. Analyze the trend and record your observations.
